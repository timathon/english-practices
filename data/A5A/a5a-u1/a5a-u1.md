## **Listening Scripts**

### **P9 - Activity Three: Listen and match.**

What snacks do you like? Chocolate, chips, biscuits, candies, or fruit? We asked 50 primary school students.

17 students love chips. 13 students like biscuits, 12 love chocolate, and eight like candies.

The snacks high in fat, salt, and sugar are their favorites. Too many of these snacks are not good for us. One bar of chocolate has about 10 teaspoons of sugar. A bag of chips every day is like drinking five bottles of oil a year.

So next time you have a snack, make healthy choices.
