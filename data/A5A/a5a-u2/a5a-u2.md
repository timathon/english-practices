How do we recycle milk boxes?

**Milk box magic**
Dear all,
Each of us has a box of milk every day at school. We use over 150,000 milk boxes every year! But many of us just throw them away.
Milk boxes have three parts: paper, plastic and metal. Each part can turn into something new, like newspapers, bottles and cans.
It is easy to recycle a milk box in just three steps:

1. Open the milk box and wash it with water.
2. Make it flat.
3. Put it into a recycling box.
Now the milk box moves to a new beginning! Let's start now for a green world!
Students from Class 502
