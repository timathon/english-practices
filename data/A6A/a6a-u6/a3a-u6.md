**History: Famous travellers**

### 1 Look and say.

What do you know about the people in the pictures?

* Marco Polo

### 2 Read and answer.

There were many great travellers in the past. People today love their stories. They helped us learn a lot.

In 138 BC, Zhang Qian travelled from Chang'an (today's Xi'an) to the west. His trips took him across huge deserts and over high mountains. He went to different places and learnt about their cultures. His trips helped the opening of the Silk Road.

Marco Polo was famous for his travel on the Silk Road. He was one of the first Europeans to visit China. He came to China in 1275 and stayed for 17 years. He wrote about his exciting life in China at the time.

1. Why were Zhang Qian's trips important?
2. How long did Marco Polo stay in China?
3. What do you think of Zhang Qian and Marco Polo?
