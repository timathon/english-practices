**Lu Ban and the umbrella**

* **Grandpa:** Are you going out, Lingling?
* **Lingling:** Yes, Grandpa. I'm visiting Amy this afternoon.
* **Grandpa:** It's raining. Take an umbrella.
* **Lingling:** Grandpa, who first got the idea of making an umbrella?
* **Grandpa:** No one knows for sure. But stories say it was Lu Ban. One day, Lu Ban was on the way home. It rained. He thought, "What can I make to keep off the rain?" Just then, he saw a boy with a big leaf over his head. "That's a good idea!" Lu Ban said. He ran back home and started to work. First, he used bamboo to make the sticks. Next, he cut some cloth. Then, he put the cloth over the sticks. That was the first umbrella!
* **Lingling:** Wow! Lu Ban had great ideas and clever hands! Thanks to him, I can use an umbrella today.
