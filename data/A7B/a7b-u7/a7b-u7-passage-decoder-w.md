### Unit 7 自测卷 Reading Comprehension: 1. A Family Camping Trip

Hello, I'm Beth. Last weekend I went on a camping trip with my family. It was also my mum's birthday. My grandparents, uncles, aunts and cousins came and joined us. We went to the countryside. My cousin Max is a food influencer. He often makes videos about cooking at home. So this time, he took many cooking things and food. We put up our tents. Max made a fire and cooked for us! Max cooked beef, potatoes, tofu, mutton and fish. He made our day! There were also a lot of sweets and a cake for my mum. Everything looked delicious. I had some beef and potatoes first. When I tried to get some cake, I found there was no cake left and there were only some sweets. I was too late! Luckily, Susan had a big piece. I had some of hers. Thanks, @Susan! You are so nice a cousin!

Question 1: What did Max do on the camping trip?
Answer: He cooked for the family.

Question 2: What did Beth eat on the camping trip?
Answer: She ate beef, potatoes, and cake.

Question 3: What does the underlined word "hers" refer to?
Answer: Susan's cake.

Question 4: The material is mainly about?
Answer: A camping trip with family.

### Unit 7 自测卷 Reading Comprehension: 2. How Was Your Weekend

How was your weekend? People often do different things on the weekend. Let's see what these four students did last weekend and how they felt.

David: It was not bad. I went out with my friends. We went to the cinema and saw an interesting film. Then we went to a new restaurant and ate a big meal. The food was good. We talked about our favourite sports happily.

Jill: My weekend was not very exciting. It was raining and cold, so I didn't go out. On Sunday morning I just stayed at home and watched TV. And in the afternoon I did my homework and read some books.

Mark: It was great. My friend and I went dancing. I had a lot of fun. I made some new friends. They were very friendly.

Anna: I thought my weekend was terrible. It was my good friend Leo's birthday last Saturday. I forgot it, so I didn't buy him a present. He called me and invited me to his home. Question 5: What did David talk about with his friends?
Answer: Their favourite sports.

Question 6: Why did Jill stay at home last weekend?
Answer: Because the weather was bad.

Question 7: What does the underlined word "except" mean in Chinese?
Answer: 除了.

Question 8: Which of the following is TRUE?
Answer: Leo had a birthday party last Saturday.

### Unit 7 自测卷 Reading Comprehension: 3. A Visit to the Farm

Tom, Jim and Ted are cousins. They study in the same school. They usually go to the countryside to see their grandparents at weekends. Their grandparents have a farm. Last weekend, Tom, Jim and Ted went to the farm to see their grandparents by bike. They met at Tom's home on Saturday morning and set out at 2:00 in the afternoon. It is a long way from Tom's home to the farm. They took a lot of food and drinks such as bread, water, eggs, bananas and so on. They got to their grandparents' farm three hours later. Their grandparents were happy. The next day, Tom, Jim and Ted helped with the farm work in the morning. They kept the chickens and then they watered the vegetables. At noon, their grandmother cooked fish and many vegetables for them. She cooked well. Jim didn't like vegetables at all in the past. But now, those vegetables were so nice and he liked them a lot. In the afternoon, their grandfather helped them to fly kites. Their kites flew high. What an interesting country life! They all had a wonderful weekend.

Question 9: Tom, Jim and Ted are?
Answer: Students.

Question 10: They got to the farm at?
Answer: 5:00 p.m.

Question 11: What did they do to help their grandparents?
Answer: Kept the chickens and watered the vegetables.

Question 12: Which of the following can be the best title of the passage?
Answer: A Happy Weekend.
