A Week to Remember

**Saturday, 28 March**

Today is the final day of our **six-day trip** to the practice base. It has been a long but amazing journey!

During the week, we experienced many things that we don't have in school. At first, the **military training** was very tiring. We had to stand still for a long time under the sun, but it taught me how to be brave and strong. In the following days, we learned to cook simple meals and even planted some trees. My favorite activity was the **team-building games**. They taught us that "Unity is strength."

Last night, we had a big campfire party. We talked and laughed until late. This trip made me more independent. I can now fold my quilt neatly and take care of myself. Although I feel a bit tired, I have learned so much. It was truly a week to remember!
