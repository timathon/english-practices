### 七、书面表达（共20分）

47. 请以“A Day to Remember”为题，写一篇英语短文，描述你生活中难忘的一天。
提示：
(1) 简要说明这是怎样的一天（时间、天气等）；
(2) 描述这一天发生的主要事情；
(3) 阐述为什么这一天让你难以忘怀。

要求：

(1) 文中要包含所给的提示信息，可适当增加细节；

(2) 词数70~80；

(3) 要求字迹工整，语句通顺，条理清晰；

(4) 文中不得出现真实的学校、班级和姓名。

**A Day to Remember** _______________________________________________________________________________



### **Model Essay**
**A Day to Remember** Last Sunday was a warm and sunny day I'll never forget. I went to the park with my family. We flew a kite on the grass and had a picnic near the lake. I took many nice photos of my family with my new camera. In the afternoon, we helped an old lady find her lost cat. She thanked us a lot. This day is unforgettable because I had a happy time with my family and learned that helping others brings great joy.
