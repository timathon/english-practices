**注意：**
(1) 词数 60–80;
(2) 文中不能出现真实的学校、班级及姓名。

**My First Day in Middle School**

* 49. **例文:**


> **My First Day in Middle School**
> My first day in middle school was exciting and I was a little afraid! Everything looked so fresh—new buildings, a huge playground, and many new faces. I walked into my classroom and met my classmates. We smiled and said hello to each other. My teacher walked in and greeted us warmly. She told us about our class rules and subjects. Everyone was so friendly. I felt happy and couldn't wait to learn more here. What a great start!
