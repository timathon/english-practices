### 五、书面表达

45. *例文:*

> **My Most Special Day**
> Last Sunday was my most special day. It was my birthday. My parents had a small party for me at home. They cooked my favourite food and bought a nice cake. My best friends came and gave me lovely gifts. We sang, danced and played games happily. I felt very excited and loved. It was a day full of joy and I will never forget it.
