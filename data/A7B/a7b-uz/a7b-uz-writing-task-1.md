### **Model Essay**

**Dear Editor,**
**I think my friend Steve is one of the Sunshine teenagers in our school. Let me tell you something about him.**
Steve is a thirteen-year-old boy who comes from Shanghai. He is in Class 7, Grade 7. He is kind and friendly. He always gets on well with his classmates. He is also helpful. He usually goes to the old people's home to read some newspapers to the old people, clean the rooms and do some washing. Steve is a hard-working student, too. He works hard on all the subjects. He is good at playing football. He hopes he can get into the school team. He likes animals. He keeps a pet dog.
Do you think Steve is a Sunshine teenager?
**Yours,**
**Li Hua**
