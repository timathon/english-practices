**Special difficulties 难点** **Words often confused 经常容易混淆的词** Receive and Take. 

* **receive:** I have just received a letter from my brother. 我刚从我弟弟那里收到一封信。 


* **take:** Someone has taken my pen. 有人拿走了我的钢笔。take是“拿走”的意思。
