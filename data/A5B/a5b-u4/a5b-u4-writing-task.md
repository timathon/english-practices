## Writing Task

### Writing Prompt


**十四、请根据题目要求写出不少于五句话的文段。**

**同学们，古代的人们用智慧和汗水创造了许多伟大的奇迹。现在请你以 My Journey along the Silk Road 为题写一个文段，设想一下如果你是一名踏上丝绸之路的少年商人，你会有怎样的经历呢？**

---

**下面的问题可以帮助你构思。**

* **Where did you go?**
* **What did you carry and trade? (e.g., silk, tea)**
* **What did you see on the way? (e.g., different places, people)**
* **How did you feel about the journey?**

### Model Essay
**My Journey along the Silk Road**

I was a young merchant on the Silk Road. I started my trip in Chang’an. I put silk and tea on my camel. People liked these things.

I saw big deserts and tall mountains. The nights were cold and the stars were bright. The roads were hard to walk on. I met traders, nomads, and friendly villagers. We shared food and stories. We traded things.

The trip was long and sometimes hard, but it was fun. I learned a lot and made new friends. Travelling along the Silk Road was a great adventure. I will always remember it.
