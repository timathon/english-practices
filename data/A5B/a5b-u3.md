Listening Script for P33 
Fuel up - Activity 3: Listen and choose. What was Ren Rongrong's hope for children? Do you know Ren Rongrong? He was the writer of Mei Tou Mao and Bu Gao Xing. He was born on the 19th of May, 1923, in Shanghai. 
He enjoyed telling stories to children, and he hoped they could love reading. So, he wrote many books for them. And he turned many English stories into Chinese, so children in China could enjoy them. 
His books made many children happy. Sadly, he passed away in 2022. People will always remember him and his great stories.
