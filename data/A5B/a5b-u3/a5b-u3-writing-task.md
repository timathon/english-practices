### Model Essay

**Book Report**

* **Title:** Charlotte's Web
* **Writer:** E.B. White
* **What happened in the book?**
The story was about a pig named Wilbur and a spider named Charlotte. Wilbur was in danger, but Charlotte helped him by writing messages in her web. This made Wilbur famous and saved his life. In the end, Charlotte was dead, but her children stayed on the farm with Wilbur.
* **Do you like this book? Why or why not?**
Yes, I like it. It shows true friendship and bravery. Charlotte is very kind and smart.
* **What is your favourite part of the book?**
My favourite part is when Charlotte writes “SOME PIG” in her web. It shows she is clever and caring.
