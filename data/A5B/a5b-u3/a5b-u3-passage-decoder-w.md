### Unit 3 Hit it big, Wrap up & Let's explore: 2. World Book Day

Do you know why people celebrate World Book Day? World Book Day is a special day for everyone who loves reading.  It takes place on April 23rd each year. This date is very important because it is the date of death of William Shakespeare, a famous English writer. People all over the world read his poems and watch his plays. We also remember another great writer Miguel de Cervantes from Spain on this day. He wrote the interesting book Don Quixote about a brave knight. These great writers gave us wonderful stories that we still enjoy today. On this day, many schools and libraries hold interesting activities. Children often dress up as their favourite book characters and some classes create their own little books. Many students become "reading buddies" where older students read interesting stories to younger students. After reading, they make beautiful bookmarks together and share their favourite books in class. Students also like to exchange books with friends or give bookmarks as gifts. Books are like magic keys that can open doors to new worlds, helping us learn, dream and grow. 

Question 1: Why do people celebrate World Book Day on April 23rd? Answer: Because it is the date of death of William Shakespeare and Miguel de Cervantes. 
Question 2: What do "reading buddies" do? Answer: Read stories to younger students. 
Question 3: What do students often make to give as gifts on this day? Answer: Beautiful bookmarks. 
Question 4: What is true about the activities on World Book Day? Answer: They share their favourite books. 
Question 5: What does the passage mean when it says "Books are like magic keys"? Answer: Books help us learn about new things.

### Unit 3 Formative Practice: 13. Book Report: Charlotte's Web

Title: Charlotte's Web
Writer: E.B. White
What happened in the book?
Wilbur was a little pig. He lived on a farm. He felt very lonely. Then he met a spider named Charlotte. They became good friends. Charlotte was very smart. She wrote words "SOME PIG" in her web to save Wilbur. The words in the web surprised everyone and made them talked about the little pig Wilbur. People thought Wilbur was special. Yes, I do. It is a story about friendship and helping others. It made me feel happy and sad.

I liked when Charlotte wrote "SOME PIG" in her web. It was clever and surprising.
