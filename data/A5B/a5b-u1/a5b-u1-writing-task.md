### Model Essay

**My Story of Growth**

Last summer, I went to a summer camp. At first, I was nervous because I didn't know anyone there. But I decided to be brave and make new friends. I talked to other kids and played games with them. By the end of the camp, I had many new friends and felt much more confident. I learned that being brave and friendly can help me overcome my fears. It made me feel good and proud of myself.
