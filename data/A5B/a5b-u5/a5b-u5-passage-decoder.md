### Unit 5 Fuel up: 1. Help Help Help

When you get lost in the wild, don't worry. Stay calm and think before you move. Here are some ways to help you. Shout "help" three times, then repeat. It helps people find you. Next, look for water. Drop stones on the ground so you can go back. Also, look at the sun to find directions. It rises in the east and sets in the west. If you feel cold, collect dry leaves and branches to stay warm. You can also make a big mark with stones or sticks.

### Unit 5 Fuel up: 2. Finding Directions in Nature

Sometimes you can check the following tips to find directions. Tree branches grow towards the sun. In the northern part of the world, trees usually have more branches on the south side because it gets more sunlight. Moss grows on hard, dark, and wet places. So it often grows on the north side of trees because the north side is dark and wet. If you don't see moss on trees, check rocks. Moss grows on both trees and rocks. Ants also give clues. They often build their homes on the south side of trees. This side is warm and safe. By watching branches, moss, and ants carefully, you can find directions in nature.
