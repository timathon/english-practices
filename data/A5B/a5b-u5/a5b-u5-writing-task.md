## Writing Task

### Writing Prompt

**十四、写作。**
**同学们，你们有没有像文彦博一样，充满智慧地解决自己遇到的问题，或帮助他人解决问题的经历呢？请你以“A Clever Way Out”为题，写一篇不少于 5 句话的短文。以下是一些提示，希望能帮助你构思：**

* **Where were you?**
* **When did it happen?**
* **Who were there?**
* **What happened?**
* **What did you do?**
* **How did you feel?**



### Model Essay
**A Clever Way Out**

One sunny day, I was in the park with my little brother. We were playing with a ball. Suddenly, the ball rolled into a deep hole. My brother was very sad because he couldn't get the ball out.

I thought for a moment. Then, I had an idea. I ran to the water fountain and filled a bucket with water. I poured the water into the hole. The water made the ball float up. My brother was so happy when he saw the ball come out of the hole.

We were both very excited. I felt proud that I could help my brother. We continued playing and had a great time.
