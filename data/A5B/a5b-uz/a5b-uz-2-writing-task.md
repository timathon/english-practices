## Writing Task

### Writing Prompt

**十四、写作。**
**同学们，岁月流转，我们生活的城市、交通、教育、科技、娱乐方式等方方面面，都在悄悄发生着变化。但也有些东西从未改变。请你想一想，记录你身边的变与不变吧。参考问题如下：**

* **What are the differences?**
* **What did we do in the past?**
* **What do we do now?**
* **What do you think of the changes?**


### Model Essay

In the past, people walked or rode bicycles to go nearby. Now, we have many buses and subways. They are fast and can help us travel far. I think these changes are good because they make our lives easier, but sometimes I still enjoy walking with my family on sunny days for fun.
