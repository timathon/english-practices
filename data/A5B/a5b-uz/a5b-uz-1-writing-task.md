## Writing Task

### Writing Prompt

**十四、写作。**
**同学们，相信你也有过参与志愿服务或者帮助别人的经历吧！请你也写一写，分享你的故事吧。以下问题供你参考：**

* **When and where did you go?**
* **What did you do?**
* **How did you feel?**
* **What did you learn from it?**




### Model Essay

Last month, I helped clean up a park near my home. It was a Sunday morning. My friends and I picked up trash and planted some flowers. The park looked much nicer after our work. I felt happy and proud. I learned that keeping our environment clean is important and that everyone can make a difference.
