**1.口语问答**

Set 1
1. **Where did you go this spring?**
I went to a park near my home.
2. **Who went there with you?**
My mum and dad went there with me.
3. **How did you go there?**
We went there by bus.
4. **What did you do there?**
We climbed mountains and took lots of photos.
5. **How did you feel?**
I felt tired but very happy.

Set 2
6. **What is your favourite book?**
My favourite book is *Charlotte's Web*.
7. **What kind of book is it?**
It's a warm animal storybook.
8. **What do you know from the book?**
A spider helps save a cute pig's life.
9. **Why do you like this book?**
It tells me true friendship is important.
10. **What's your favourite part of this book?**
Charlotte writes words to save the pig Wilbur.

Set 3
11. **What difficulties did you face in the past?**
I lost my coat on the playground last week.
12. **What did you do to solve the problem?**
I went to the the playground and the Lost and Found. *(Note: "the the" is transcribed exactly as it appears in the original image)*
13. **How long did it take you to work it out?**
It took me the whole afternoon.
14. **How did you feel after solving the problem?**
I felt relaxed and thankful.
15. **What did you learn from it?**
I should look after my clothes carefully.

**2. 看图说话**

Set 1
Daming **went to a hill** with his friends and they had a race. At first, he was **at the front**. Then, he saw some **rubbish**. So, he stopped and **picked it up**. **In the end**, he was the last one to get to the top of the hill, but his friends thought he was **the real winner**.

Set 2
This week, something strange happened in Daming's family. At first, Mum **lost her ring**. And then, Dad **lost his car key**. Next, Daming **lost his coins**. Daming **looked around carefully** and saw something shiny in the **bird's home**! Aha, the bird **took all things away**.

Set 3
I have a little brother. When he was **ten months old**, he cried a lot and I was not happy. When he was **five**, we **played football together**. Now he is **ten**, we go to the same school and we **help each other**. It's great to **grow up** with him!
