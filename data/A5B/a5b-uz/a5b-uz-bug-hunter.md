### Unit 1: My Growth Story

Last holiday, I went to a winter camp. It was cold. We walked and ran for hours. We also did many other fun things. It was hard but great!

### Unit 2: A Helping Hand

One day, I went to a park with my mum. We saw a lot of rubbish on the grass. We picked it up. We put it into the rubbish bin. We were tired but happy.

### Unit 3: My Favourite Book

My favourite book is "The Secret Garden". It is a great story. In the story, Mary found a lovely garden. She made new friends there. This wonderful story tells us about nature and love.

### Unit 4: My Favourite Ancient Thing

I like an ancient Chinese fan best. It is beautiful and light. There are nice flowers on it. My grandma gave it to me. I love this old thing a lot.

### Unit 5: A Clever Way Out

Last Sunday, I went to the shopping centre with my mum. We met a lost girl. We asked for her dad's phone number and gave him a call. Soon her dad came to find her. The little girl felt happy again.

### Unit 6: Changes in My School

My school changed a lot. Then there wasn't a computer in the classroom. Now there is a computer in the classroom. And we also have a new library. My school is better and better.
