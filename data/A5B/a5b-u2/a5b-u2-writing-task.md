## Writing Task

### Writing Prompt

**十四、请根据题目要求写出不少于五句话的文段。**

同学们，在上面的阅读中，作者分享了他做志愿者的经历和收获。相信你也是一个乐于助人的孩子。请结合生活实际，描述一次你帮助他人或参与志愿服务的经历。以下问题可帮助你组织语言与内容：

---

### **English Text**

* **Who did you help?** (the teacher, the classmate, the friend...)
* **What did you do?**
* **When and where did it happen?**
* **How did you feel?** (proud, tired but happy...)

**The Helping Hand**

### Model Essay
One day, my classmate Lele looked worried after English class. He almost cried in the classroom, because he wanted to read the text well. I came to help. I read, then he read after me. He worked it out finally. He was happy and I felt happy too.
