### Unit 2 Hit it big, Wrap up & Let's explore: 2. Non-profit Organisations

Non-profit organisations (NPOs) are special groups. Their main goal is not to make money but to help people, animals, or the environment. They use the money they get from donations to do good things for the world. There are many different kinds of NPOs. Some, like UNICEF, work to protect children's rights and make sure they are healthy and can go to school. Some, like WWF (World Wide Fund for Nature), work to protect endangered animals and their homes. Other organisations, such as the Red Cross, help people when big disasters happen, like earthquakes or floods. Volunteers are very important to NPOs. They are people who give their time to help without getting paid. Kids can also help! They can donate their old toys and books, or even help to raise money with their parents. Everyone can do something to help NPOs make the world a better place.

Question 1: What is the main goal of non-profit organisations? Answer: To help people, animals, or the environment.
Question 2: Where do non-profit organisations get their money to help others? Answer: From donations.
Question 3: Which organisation helps to protect endangered animals? Answer: WWF.
Question 4: Who are volunteers? Answer: People who give their time to help without getting paid.
Question 5: How can kids help non-profit organisations? Answer: They can donate their old toys and books, or even help to raise money with their parents.

### Unit 2 Formative Practice 2: 13. Volunteering

Volunteering is one of my favourite ways to spend free time. I often join different volunteer groups. No matter what the activity is, I always enjoy it and encourage others to try it too. Here are my top three reasons why volunteering is so meaningful.

1. Get Experience and Skills
Although you don't get paid, volunteering helps you gain useful experience and skills for the future. For example, many volunteer tasks teach you how to work well in a team.

One of the best parts of volunteering is meeting other volunteers! Many are very interesting, and you can make new friends.

Many volunteers say volunteering makes them feel more confident. I used to be shy, but volunteering has helped me feel more sure of myself when talking to others.
