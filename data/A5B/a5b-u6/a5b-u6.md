> In the Daliang Mountains, tall green trees grow. Birds sing all around. But about 40 years ago, it was very different there.

> After 22 years, the Daliang Mountains became a green world, with over 10 million trees.
> By planting trees, Yang Shanzhou also planted hope. His hard work gave people a brighter and greener life.
