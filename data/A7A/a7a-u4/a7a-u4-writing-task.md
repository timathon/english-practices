# Writing Task: My Favourite School Day

## Prompt
Write a short passage about your favourite school day. Describe your schedule, the subjects you have, which one is your favourite, and why you like it.

## Requirements
- Mention at least 4 school subjects.
- Use connectors like "and", "but", and "because".
- Describe how you feel about the day.
- Word count: 60-80 words.
