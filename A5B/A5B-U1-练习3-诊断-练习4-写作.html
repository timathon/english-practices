Part 6: Reading A - Culture & Life (15 points)

Read the passage and choose the best answer.

First Day of School in Germany

The first day of primary school is a very big day for children in Germany. It is called "Einschulung". On this day, children do not carry heavy schoolbags. Instead, they carry a "Schultüte".

What is a Schultüte? It is a large cone made of paper or plastic. It looks like a giant ice cream cone! Parents or grandparents give it to the children. Inside the cone, there are no ice creams. There are sweets, chocolates, toys, and school things like pencils and erasers.

The children are very excited. They take photos with their Schultüte. It helps them feel happy and brave on their first day.

  1. What is a "Schultüte"?
    A. A schoolbag.    B. A large cone.    C. An ice cream.

  2. Who gives the Schultüte to the children?
    A. Teachers.    B. Parents or grandparents.    C. Other students.

  3. What is INSIDE the cone?
    A. Ice cream and cake.    B. Books and homework.    C. Sweets and pencils.
Part 7: Reading B - How Do We Grow? (15 points)

Read the passage and write T (True) or F (False).

Every living thing grows. Trees grow. Animals grow. And you grow, too. But how do you grow?

First, your bones get longer. When you were a baby, your legs were very short. Now they are longer because your bones grew. To make your bones strong, you need calcium (钙). Milk has a lot of calcium.

Second, your body needs energy to grow. You get energy from food. You should eat meat, vegetables, and fruit.

Third, sleep is very important. Your body grows a lot when you are sleeping. If you want to be tall and strong, you must sleep well.

  1. [   ] Only animals and people grow. Trees do not grow.
  2. [   ] Milk is good for your bones because it has calcium.
  3. [   ] You grow a lot when you are watching TV.

Part 8: Writing Samples (作文范文)

Sample 1 (Basic Level - 基础篇):
Hello, my name is Li Ming. Two years ago, I was eight years old. I was short and thin. I was shy in class. I couldn't play football well. But now, I am ten. I am tall and strong. I am brave. I can play football very well. I have many friends. Growing up is happy.
Sample 2 (Advanced Level - 提升篇):
My name is Wang Fang. There are many changes in my life. Three years ago, I was a primary school student in Grade 2. I was very naughty and noisy. I wasn't able to help my mom. I couldn't cook.
Now, I am in Grade 5. I am a polite girl. I study hard at school. On the weekend, I can help my mom wash clothes. I can make noodles too. I am happy to grow up.
