1. out of breath/ˌaʊt əv ˈbreθ/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 上气不接下气，喘不过气来Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: breath vs breathe
"Why are you out of breath? Have you been running?"
🧠 核心记忆法: 跑得太快，气(breath)都跑出去(out)了。

2. have a break/hæv ə breɪk/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 休息一下Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: break vs bread
"I've been working hard. Now I'm going to have a break."
🧠 核心记忆法: break有“打断”的意思，打断工作就是休息。

3. take advice/teɪk ədˈvaɪs/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 采纳建议Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: advice vs advise
"I've been thinking about what you said and I've decided to take your advice."
🧠 核心记忆法: take是拿走，拿走别人的建议并使用它。

4. recently/ˈriːsntli/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 最近Click to show
🎵 音节类型: re-cent-ly
🔍 易混辨析: recently vs recently
"Chris hasn't been feeling well recently."
🧠 核心记忆法: recent(最近的) + ly(后缀) = 最近。

5. South America/ˌsaʊθ əˈmerɪkə/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 南美洲Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: South vs North
"Laura has been travelling in South America for the last three months."
🧠 核心记忆法: South(南) + America(美洲)。

6. disturb/dɪˈstɜːb/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 打扰，打断Click to show
🎵 音节类型: dis-turb
🔍 易混辨析: disturb vs disturb
"Don't disturb me now. I'm working."
🧠 核心记忆法: dis(分开) + turb(搅动) -> 搅动思绪，即打扰。

7. hurry up/ˈhʌri ʌp/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 赶快，快点Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: hurry vs hairy
"Hurry up! We're waiting."
🧠 核心记忆法: hurry(赶紧) + up(向上/程度) -> 快快快。

8. dirty/ˈdɜːti/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 脏的Click to show
🎵 音节类型: dir-ty
🔍 易混辨析: dirty vs dairy
"Jane's little boy comes into the house with a very dirty face."
🧠 核心记忆法: dirt(泥土) + y(形容词后缀) -> 像泥土一样的，即脏的。

9. situation/ˌsɪtʃuˈeɪʃn/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 情况，处境Click to show
🎵 音节类型: sit-u-a-tion
🔍 易混辨析: situation vs station
"Study this example situation."
🧠 核心记忆法: sit(坐/位子) + uation -> 处于某个位子的状态，即情况。

10. activity/ækˈtɪvəti/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 活动Click to show
🎵 音节类型: ac-tiv-i-ty
🔍 易混辨析: activity vs active
"We use the present perfect continuous for an activity that has recently stopped."
🧠 核心记忆法: act(行动) + ivity -> 行动的表现，即活动。

11. ground/ɡraʊnd/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 地面Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Closed
🔍 易混辨析: ground vs grand
"No, but the ground is wet."
🧠 核心记忆法: 在地(ground)上跑(run)。

12. agree with/əˈɡriː wɪð/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 同意Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: agree vs degree
"I've been talking to Amanda and she agrees with me."
🧠 核心记忆法: a + gree(一致) + with -> 和某人一致，即同意。

13. tennis player/ˈtenɪs ˈpleɪə/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 网球运动员Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: player vs prayer
"Silvia is a very good tennis player."
🧠 核心记忆法: tennis(网球) + player(玩的人/运动员)。

14. how long/haʊ lɒŋ/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 多长时间Click to show
🎵 音节类型: Phrase
🔍 易混辨析: long vs along
"How long have you been learning English?"
🧠 核心记忆法: how(如何) + long(长的/久) -> 问多久。

15. panting/ˈpæntɪŋ/

🇨🇳 中文释义: 气喘吁吁的Click to show
🎵 音节类型: pan-ting
🔍 易混辨析: panting vs painting
