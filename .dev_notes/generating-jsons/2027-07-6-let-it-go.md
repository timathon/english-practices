### 2. JSON Files Generated
The following files were created in `data/E-LYRICS/l/let-it-go/`:
- **`let-it-go-vocab-guide.json`**: Extracted 5 primary vocabulary terms and 9 extra difficult words/phrases with IPA, syllable types, comparisons, and mnemonics.
- **`let-it-go-vocab-master.json`**: Generated exactly 40 questions (grouped into 4 challenges of 10 questions each) covering Cloze, Cn2En, and En2Cn types with 6 options.
- **`let-it-go-spelling-hero.json`**: Extracted all single-syllable and multi-syllable words and split them into chunks with phonetic/visual trap distractors.
- **`let-it-go-sentence-architect.json`**: Produced exactly 5 challenges of 10 sentences each (50 sentences total) with distractors, grammar clues, and accepted word-order variations.
- **`let-it-go-recall-map.json`**: Built a hierarchical mindmap covering the movie story, vocabulary categories, and grammar rules.
- **`let-it-go-text-navigator.json`**: Created detailed interactive reading trees with a dedicated "Lyrics" section and a "Movie Story Context" section.
- **`let-it-go-passage-decoder-s.json`**: Structured verbatim line-by-line translations and multiple-choice questions for both "Movie Story Context" and a dedicated "Lyrics" section.
- **`let-it-go-grammar-wizard.json`**: Generated 20 multiple-choice questions focusing on `Can & Can't` and `Don't` usage, purpose, formation, definition, and differentiation.

### 3. Verification & Clean-up
- Created and executed a Python validation script (`verify_jsons.py`) to confirm all 8 generated files exist and contain valid JSON syntax. All files successfully passed verification.
