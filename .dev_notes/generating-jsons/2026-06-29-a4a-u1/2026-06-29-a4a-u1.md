# Dev Notes: Generating Exercise JSONs for A4A Unit 1

- **Date:** 2026-06-29
- **Target Textbook/Unit:** Grade 4 Semester 1 - Unit 1 (A4A-U1)
- **Source File:** `data/A4A/a4a-u1/a4a-u1.md`

## 1. Process Overview
To convert the markdown data of `a4a-u1.md` into the interactive exercises, the transformation was automated using a Python generation script (`data/A4A/a4a-u1/generate.py`). This ensured high accuracy, correct phonetics/syllables, and proper formatting according to `GEMINI.md`.

## 2. Generated Artifacts & Implementations

### Vocab Guide (`a4a-u1-vocab-guide.json`)
- **Extraction:** All 29 appendix vocabulary items were extracted.
- **Extensions:** Added 10 extra unit-relevant phrases/words (e.g., `fast`, `swim`, `well`, `come on`, `do it`, `run fast`, `jump high`, `play ping-pong`, `jump far`, `sports star`).
- **Phonetics & Syllables:** Annotated with standard British IPA, visual/phonetic comparisons (e.g., `sport vs port`), syllable patterns (e.g., `r控制音节 (R-Controlled)`, multi-syllable hyphenation), and Chinese memorization mnemonics.

### Vocab Master (`a4a-u1-vocab-master.json`)
- **Size:** Capped at 50 questions (5 challenges of 10 questions each).
- **Categories:** Shuffled options of 6 categories to ensure high-quality, relevant distractors.
- **Hints:** Chinese prompts are added to Cloze sentences (e.g., `(提示: [Chinese meaning])`).

### Spelling Hero (`a4a-u1-spelling-hero.json`)
- **Filter:** Skipped phrases containing spaces.
- **Logic:** Single-syllable words were split by phonics graphemes (e.g., `sport` -> `sp / or / t`), and multi-syllables by standard syllables (e.g., `vol-ley-ball`). Shuffled distractors are generated for every chunk.

### Sentence Architect (`a4a-u1-sentence-architect.json`)
- **Volume:** Exactly 50 items (5 challenges of 10 sentences each).
- **Passcode:** `PCSAT` (derived from challenge titles: *Play to Win*, *Can Terry Fox Run*, *Story of Hope*, *Animal Sports Stars*, *Talk about Abilities*).
- **Details:** Includes custom noise words (non-conflicting), alternate acceptable answers, and a vocabulary IPA dictionary.

### Recall Map (`a4a-u1-recall-map.json`)
- **Tree Structure:** Nested mindmap summarizing stories (*Yes, I can!* & *Run for Hope*), vocabulary (nouns, verbs, phrases), and grammar focus (*can/can't* & *because/because of*).

### Text Navigator (`a4a-u1-text-navigator.json`)
- **Consolidation:** Merged *Start Up* and *Speed Up* text lines into a single JSON file.
- **Questions:** Added true/false statement validation for every sentence, complete with translations, notes, and keyword tags.

### Grammar Wizard (`a4a-u1-grammar-wizard.json`)
- **Structure:** 20 questions split into 2 challenges:
  - Challenge 1: `can` / `can't` for ability.
  - Challenge 2: `because` / `because of` for reason clauses vs. noun phrases.

### Passage Decoder (`a4a-u1-passage-decoder-s.json`)
- **Format:** Student's Book sentences mapped with speaker tags, correct line endings, custom vocabulary highlight lists, and 3 clean translation options.
