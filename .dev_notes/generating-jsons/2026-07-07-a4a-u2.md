### 2. JSON Files Generated
The following files were created in `data/A4A/a4a-u2/`:
- **`a4a-u2-vocab-guide.json`**: Contains all vocabulary and extra expressions with British IPA, parts of speech, meanings, context sentences, page numbers, syllable types, comparison targets, and mnemonic hooks.
- **`a4a-u2-vocab-master.json`**: Features exactly 5 challenges of 10 questions each (50 questions total) using Cloze, Cn2En, and En2Cn types with 6 options and randomized answer indices.
- **`a4a-u2-spelling-hero.json`**: Splits single-word vocabulary items into phonics/syllable chunks with visual/phonetic traps for each chunk.
- **`a4a-u2-sentence-architect.json`**: Comprises exactly 5 challenges of 10 sentences each (50 sentences total) with BrE spelling, noise word distractors, and grammar clues.
- **`a4a-u2-recall-map.json`**: Maps out Unit 2 themes, vocabulary classes, and grammar patterns in a tree structure.
- **`a4a-u2-text-navigator.json`**: Groups verbatim reading sentences from Start Up, Story Time, and Fuel Up into thematic mindmap nodes with translations, grammar statements, keywords, and highlights.
- **`a4a-u2-grammar-wizard.json`**: Tests "May I...?" / "Can you...?" and chore vocabulary across exactly 20 multiple-choice questions (2 challenges of 10).
- **`a4a-u2-passage-decoder-s.json`**: Provides sentence-by-sentence reading navigation with 3-option translation multiple-choice questions and vocabulary highlights.
